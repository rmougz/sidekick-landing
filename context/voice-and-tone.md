# Voice & Tone — Sidekick Accounting

Derived from the Agency Profit Audit landing copy and client testimonials. Match
this when writing any Sidekick lead magnet.

## The voice in one line

A specialist CFO-for-agencies who has seen inside 50+ agency P&Ls and tells you
plainly what is leaking profit - confident, concrete, no hype, no jargon.

## Markers to hit

- **First-person and from inside the work.** "What I see again and again...",
  "Rayhaan and the team". Earned observation, not repackaged theory.
- **Specific numbers over vague claims.** "+19.5% average margin", "77.25% net
  margin", "$154K/month of extra cash", "£250K+ of new profit". Odd, real numbers
  read as true; round vague ones read as marketing.
- **Outcome-led, founder-facing.** Speak to the agency owner about profit, margin,
  cash, and clarity - not about accounting mechanics.
- **Calm authority, no pressure.** The audit is "45 minutes. No pitch." Confidence
  comes from results, not urgency tactics.
- **Diagnosis framing.** "Want to see where your profit is leaking?" The reader
  self-recognises a problem; we name the structural cause.

## Hard rules

- **No em dashes.** Use a hyphen ( - ) or "..." instead. (This applies to every
  asset; it is the tell that separates hand-written from generated copy.)
- **No filler superlatives.** Avoid "transform", "unlock", "game-changer",
  "revolutionary", "supercharge". Name the specific thing instead.
- **No hedging.** Cut "kind of", "sort of", "perhaps", "maybe". Take a position.
- **Mind the currency.** Clients span £ and $ - quote each client in the currency
  used in their result (don't silently convert). UK English spelling elsewhere
  (optimise, organise, colour).

## Recurring vocabulary (use these, they're "on brand")

margins / net margin / profit leak / client-level (and pod-level) profitability /
cost-to-serve / financial layer / financial infrastructure / forward-looking /
capacity planning / data-led decisions / clarity.

## Sample lines (tone reference)

- "The average agency we work with improves margins by 19.5% - without adding a single new client."
- "45 minutes. No pitch. You'll leave with a clear financial picture of your business."
- "Decisions made on gut feel" → "Hiring against real capacity data."
