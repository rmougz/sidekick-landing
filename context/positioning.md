# Positioning — the Asset-Grade Agency (north star)

The single organising idea behind everything Sidekick does for clients. Weave it
through every lead magnet and asset as the end goal.

## The north star

Sidekick's job is to take an agency from being **a high-paying job that depends on
its founder** to an **Asset-Grade Agency**: a business that produces predictable
profit a stranger could own and run without the founder in the chair.

Asset-grade is the *outcome* of the financial infrastructure Sidekick builds. It
matters even if the founder never sells - because the same qualities that make an
agency sellable (predictable margins, owner-independence, clean forward-looking
financials) are what make the founder free, calm, and wealthy *now*. **Build it
like you'll sell it, even if you never do.**

## The maturity ladder (reusable across assets)

Every agency sits somewhere on this ladder. Naming where a reader is - and the
next rung - is a recurring diagnostic device.

| Rung | Archetype | Reality |
|------|-----------|---------|
| 1 | **The Self-Employed Job** | The business *is* the founder. ~Worthless without them. |
| 2 | **The Operator-Run Agency** | Runs on the founder's daily input. Sellable only at a discount, if at all. |
| 3 | **The Managed Agency** | Runs without the founder day-to-day; real gaps remain before a clean exit. |
| 4 | **The Asset-Grade Agency** | Predictable profit a stranger could own. Buyer-ready, premium multiple, founder optional. |

## What makes an agency asset-grade (the value drivers)

1. **Owner-independence** - delivery, sales, and key relationships don't run through the founder.
2. **Margin quality + visibility** - real-time, client-level profitability; predictable net margin.
3. **Revenue quality** - recurring/retainer over one-off project work; no dangerous client concentration.
4. **Diligence-readiness** - clean, accurate, normalised financials a buyer can trust on day one.
5. **Forward-looking layer** - capacity-based hiring, cash forecasting, decisions made on data not gut.

These map onto Sidekick's three financial layers (bookkeeping -> forward-looking
-> strategic/exit). See [[proof-assets]] (incl. the two real exits) and
[[brand-kit]] for how to present it.

- Use "asset-grade agency" as recurring on-brand vocabulary (see [[voice-and-tone]]).
